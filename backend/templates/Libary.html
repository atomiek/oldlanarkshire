About the Library

The Old College Lanarkshire library provides students with access to a wide range of academic resources, including textbooks, digital materials, study spaces, and research support. The Old College Lanarkshire library supports all subject areas taught at the college.

Facilities

  • Quiet study areas
  • Computer access
  • Printing and scanning
  • Online journals and e-books
  • Study support and research help
